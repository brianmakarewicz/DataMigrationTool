Why it's portable

The fixture creates fresh, prefix-stamped agreements but borrows every reference. One read-only discovery query finds a real procurement BU, buyer, supplier, active purchasing site, and currency already on the target pod, then stamps them in. No dependency on data we loaded earlier — it runs on a pod we've never touched.
